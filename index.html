About the Workshop

The AICTE-VAANI 2025 Workshop is a national-level initiative focused on empowering learners and educators in the fields of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. Its unique mission is to bridge the gap between advanced technologies and regional languages, particularly Tamil.

Through engaging expert talks, hands-on sessions, and case studies, this workshop sheds light on multilingual AI models, NLP, and the ethical and practical challenges of integrating AI into India’s diverse language ecosystem.

Jointly organized by the Department of Computer Applications and Department of Management Studies at Shrimati Indira Gandhi College, Trichy, and backed by AICTE and ATAL Academy, this workshop aims to create inclusive AI solutions for tomorrow.

Registration Process

Follow these 7 steps to complete your registration for the AICTE VAANI 2025 Workshop.
You can also download the full instructions as PDF.

Step 1

Visit the official ATAL Registration Portal and sign up using your personal and institutional details.

Step 2

Login using the registered email and password.

Step 3

Fill in your profile with accurate academic and professional details.

Step 4

Open the FDP section to browse available workshops.

Step 5

Use the following filters to locate the FDP:

  • FDP Title / Keyword: VAANI
  • Month: August
  • Thrust Area: Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Step 6

Click the “+” icon to apply for the workshop.

Step 7

Upload the signed NOC from your college to complete the registration.
